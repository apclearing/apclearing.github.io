Mountain West Digital Library

MWDL provides free access to over 960,000 resources from universities, colleges, public libraries, museums, historical societies, and government agencies, counties, and municipalities in Utah, Nevada, Idaho, Hawaii, and other parts of the U.S. West.

Site: http://mwdl.org/

Mountain West Digital Library Dublin Core Application Profile

Created by the Mountain West Digital Library (MWDL) Metadata Working Group in 2009, this profile superseded the Metadata Guidelines for the Mountain West Digital Library (2006).  It is intended to guide the creation of new metadata (i.e., created after June 1, 2010) by members and contributing partners of the MWDL.
